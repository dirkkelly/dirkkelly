Hello, I'm Dirk Kelly.

I want to live in a society where we all have input over the decisions that affect us, where we all share custodianship over our finite resources, and where we all strive to look after each other.

I don't think that a small handful of people should control all the wealth and power in our society and decide our fate under a changing climate and collapsing economy.

I think a revolution is necessary, in progress, and achievable if we work together to abandon all the systems supporting this unjust society.

We can do this by creating the necessary replacements of these manifestations of power in a manner which is collaborative, equitable, and cares for life in our ecosystem.
